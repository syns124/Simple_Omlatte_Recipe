Simple Omlatte Recipe

An easy and quick dish,perfect for any meal. This classic omelette combines beaten eggs cooked to perfection,opthional filled with your choice of cheese,vegetables,or meats,

Prepration Time

  • Total:Approximately 10 minutes
  • Preparation:5 minutes
  • Cooking:5 minutes

Ingredients

  • 2-3 large eggs
  • Salt to taste
  • Pepper to taste
  • 1 tablespoon of butter or oil
  • Optional filling: cheese,diced vegetables,cooked meats,herbs

Instructions

  1. Beat the eggs:In a blowthe eggs with a pinch of salt and pepper units they are well mixed,You can add a tablespoon of water or milk for a fluffier texture.
  2. Heat the pan:Place a non-stick frying pan over medium heat and add butter or oil.
  3. Cook the omelette:Once the butter is melted and bubbling,pour in the eggs.Tilt the pan to ensure the eggs evenly coat the ssuface.
  4. Add fillings(optional):When the eggs being to set at the esges but are still slightly runny in the middle,sprinkle your chosen fillings over one halt of the omelette.
  5. Fold and serve:As the omelette continues to cook,carefully lift one edge and fold it over the fillings.Let it cook for another minute,then slide it onto a plate.
  6. Enjoy:Serve hot,with additional salt and pepper if needed.
